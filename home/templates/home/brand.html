Colors

As an homage to relevant contributions to the information design field, the Open Visualization Academy uses sequential color palettes from ColorBrewer, color interpolation (gradients) from D3, and categorical colors from Tableau 10.

Our primary color palette is YlOrRd — which contains shades of yellow, orange and red. These colors are turned into a smooth gradient and featued in our symbol.
